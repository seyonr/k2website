MEET THE CREATIVE DIRECTOR

I'm Kaaviyan Kieren Bourassa, and I thrive on partnering with small businesses to give them valuable time while boosting their social media presence to new heights.

I've spent the last 5 years immersing myself in the world of graphic design, visual branding, and film editing. Starting from photo editing, I've gradually expanded my skills to encompass film editing, honing my expertise along the way.

After establishing my fully equipped studio, the logical progression was to assemble a dedicated team. Through meticulous selection, I've brought aboard a team of skilled professionals prepared to excel in our endeavors. With our collective talents aligned, we are ready to actualize our visions with precision and excellence.

We offer a wide range of services, with our core competency being Graphic Designing. Working closely with brands, artists, teams, and productions, we excel in creating captivating brand narratives through visually engaging content.
